Radio-Integrated Fire Helmet

A basic upgrade to firefighting technology. All firefighters carry a radio with them clipped to the outside of their fire suit, and because of the radio is always outside of their face masks, they have a hard time communicating when they most need it. Garbled audio coming in, and muffled speech going out. This device will attach to a firefighter's face mask and provide a microphone inside the mask, and a speaker over the ear, plugging into the existing handheld radio. Firefighters will be able to hear and speak to each other clearly on scene.
