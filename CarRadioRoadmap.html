Mobile FM Radio Station

Many people get into their cars every day, to go to work or school or just to drive, and have to fiddle with their phones for an entire minute just to connect it to their car or to some other bluetooth device which they use to connect their music provider to the car radio. How nice would it be to just get in and drive, and know that your music was playing anyway? The solution is this: using software-defined radio (SDR) we can create a compact computer that can play your playlists over the air, acting as a personal radio station wihtin your car (or anywhere else). Upload your music files to an SD card and insert it into the device, and it will recognie your files and play them automatically.
